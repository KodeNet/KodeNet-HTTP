Use these settings to connect to the KodeNet IRC network. The web client is always available over a secure TLS connection.

+ **host** `irc.koderoot.net`
+ **port** `6667`
+ **tls** `6697`
+ **web** [WebIRC](https://webirc.koderoot.net)
